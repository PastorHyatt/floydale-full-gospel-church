About Floydale Full Gospel

Welcome to our church, a fellowship rooted in Bible believing. We acknowledge that all people "have a pass" on account of sin, for "all have sinned, and come short of the glory of God" (Romans 3:23). But by God's glorious grace, which is "exceeding abundant with faith and love which is in Christ Jesus" (1 Timothy 1:14), we have been forgiven and chosen.

It is by His divine call that we strive to put off the old man with his "corrupt according to the deceitful lusts" (Ephesians 4:22), and earnestly endeavour to put on the new man, which "after God is created in righteousness and true holiness" (Ephesians 4:24). This transformation, given by the Spirit, empowers us to live a life pleasing unto Him who "hath chosen us in him before the foundation of the world, that we should be holy and without blame before him in love" (Ephesians 1:4).

Within our doors, you will find a church environment where everyone feels valued, supported, and inspired. We believe that "as we have many members in one body, and all members have not the same office: So we, being many, are one body in Christ, and every one members one of another" (Romans 12:4-5). We are called to "bear ye one another's burdens, and so fulfil the law of Christ" (Galatians 6:2), and to "consider one another to provoke unto love to good works" (Hebrews 10:24).

Whether you are new to faith and seeking the truth of God's Word, or if you are seeking a new church to call home, we invite you to experience the warmth and joy of our congregation. For "where two or three are gathered together in my name, there am I in the midst of them" (Matthew 18:20), and in His presence is "fulness of joy" (Psalm 16:11). Come, and let us "love one another with a pure heart fervently" (1 Peter 1:22).

Our Core Values

Our church is built upon foundational principles that guide our faith, actions, and community life. These core values shape who we are and how we serve God and one another.

  • **Bible-Centered Teaching:** We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and the ultimate authority for faith and life.
  • **Passionate Worship:** We seek to honor God through heartfelt praise, prayer, and the proclamation of His truth.
  • **Loving Community:** We are committed to fostering a welcoming and supportive environment where every member feels valued and connected.
  • **Spiritual Growth:** We encourage and equip believers to grow in their relationship with Christ through discipleship and personal study.
  • **Outreach and Service:** We are called to share the Gospel and demonstrate God's love through compassionate service to our local community and beyond.
  • **Prayer:** We believe in the power of prayer and commit to regular intercession for our church, community, and the world.

These values are reflected in everything we do, from our Sunday services to our community outreach efforts.

Church By-Laws

The By-Laws of Floydale Full Gospel provide the framework for our church's governance, operations, and membership. They ensure that our church functions in an orderly manner, upholding our mission and values.

For a detailed understanding of our church's structure, membership responsibilities, and operational procedures, please refer to the official By-Laws document.
